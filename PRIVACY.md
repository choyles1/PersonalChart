# Privacy Notes

PersonalChart is designed as a local-first personal health record prototype.

## Prototype Behavior

- The app does not include a backend server.
- The app does not create accounts.
- The app does not intentionally send health data to this project or to a cloud database.
- App data is stored in the tester's browser/device.
- Encrypted backup files are created and moved manually by the user.

## Tester Guidance

Use fictional or sample data only. Do not upload real visit documents, ID cards, insurance cards, advance directives, medication lists, or other sensitive files.

## Future Production Considerations

Before production use, the project needs a full privacy policy, security review, data retention rules, export/deletion guarantees, and legal review.
